<p>I am a data-capable immunologist passionate about leveraging -omics data to unravel the complexity of immune-relevant disease states. During my PhD, I complemented my immunology background with computational expertise to become a full-fledged bioinformatician motivated to systematically dig deeply into disease biology.</p>

<p>I am currently finishing my PhD in the <a href="https://www.bocklab.org/">Medical Epigenomics lab</a> of Christoph Bock at <a href="https://cemm.at/">CeMM</a> and the Medical University of Vienna. My project focuses on the systematic characterization of epigenetic dysregulation underlying rare monogenic immune disorders. Prior to this, I completed an Honours BSc in Microbiology and Immunology at the University of British Columbia.</p>
